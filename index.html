About Our Server

Welcome to The Limitless SMP

Founded in 2025, we've grown into a thriving community of builders, explorers, and adventurers who share a passion for Minecraft.

Server Seasons

What are Seasons?

The Limitless SMP runs on a seasonal model, where each season brings a fresh world, new features, and exciting opportunities for players. Seasons typically last 3-6 weeks, allowing everyone to experience the thrill of starting fresh while implementing community feedback from previous seasons.
